Outdoor Safety Tips

Stay safe during your outdoor escapades with these important safety tips:

  • Plan your route and share it with someone.
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out.
  • Stay hydrated and bring enough food for the trip.
  • Wear appropriate clothing and footwear.
  • Know your limits and don't take unnecessary risks.
